Meet the founder

Hi, I am Shanay Gandhi, the founder of Printify. I am 14 years old, currently in 9th grade, and I study at MGIS. I enjoy creating new ideas, designing useful products, and turning digital concepts into real 3D printed objects.

With the help and support of my parents, I was able to accomplish my dream of starting Printify and sharing my 3D printed creations with others.

I built Printify to feel premium from the first click to the final product: creative 3D printed gifts, fidgets, collectibles, and custom designs made with fair pricing, clear communication, and a polished finish.
